Click on the canvas to halt the rotation of the planets. Click again to restart the animation.

Drag the mouse across the canvas to change the rotation direction of the planets.

Use the "A", "S", and "D" keys to rotate the pivot joint of the first, second, and third cubes respectively.

Use the "F" and G" key to rotate the pivot joints of the left and right fingers backwards, respectively.

Use the "H" and J" key to rotate the pivot joints of the left and right fingers forwards, respectively.

Please us a browser that supports "canvas".
